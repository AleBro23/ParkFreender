PARK FREENDER

Simplifying Parking in Trento

We, Konstantinos Zefkilis, Alessandro Brognara, and Luca Pierno Pio, students from the University of Trento, developed ParkFreender, a user-friendly web app for convenient parking in Trento. It simplifies parking with features like mapping available spaces and directions, aiming to enhance urban mobility and make Trento more accessible.
